1. British Airways — Data Science Job Simulation — Forage

Issuing Authority: British Airways / Forage

Candidate Name: Hrithik Bhardwaj

Credential ID: eQ9r5QP9vENLTyrDb

Date: May 2026

Credential Link: Link

What I Learned:

This job simulation placed me in the role of a data scientist at British Airways, tasked with building a predictive machine learning model to proactively identify customers likely to purchase a holiday package. The goal was to shift the airline's acquisition strategy from reactive airport sales to targeted, data-driven digital outreach.

I managed the end-to-end data pipeline, starting with extensive exploratory data analysis (EDA) to uncover behavioral signals such as ancillary service preferences and schedule clustering. I preprocessed the dataset by encoding categorical variables, normalizing continuous features with StandardScaler, and implementing a strict stratified train-validation-test split. To overcome severe class imbalance, I evolved the model from a baseline Random Forest (using SMOTE synthetic oversampling) to an optimized XGBoost classifier using native class-weight penalties.

The final XGBoost model achieved a strong recall of 77%, successfully capturing the vast majority of high-value targets. Beyond modelling, I extracted feature importance metrics to identify the key drivers of holiday bookings and translated these statistical outputs into a comprehensive PowerPoint report, delivering actionable, non-technical business insights for executive stakeholders.
